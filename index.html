Have A Questions?

1 What is civil litigation?
Civil litigation is a legal process where disputes between individuals or entities are resolved in court.
2 How long does civil litigation take?
The duration varies, but civil litigation can take months to several years, depending on the complexity of the case.
3 What are the stages of civil litigation?
Civil litigation typically involves pleadings, discovery, trial, and possibly an appeal.
4 Can a civil litigation case be settled out of court?
Yes, many civil litigation cases are settled through negotiation or mediation before reaching trial.
5 What is the role of a civil litigation attorney?
A civil litigation attorney represents clients in legal disputes, guiding them through the litigation process and advocating on their behalf in court.
